Bio

Hi, I am Evgenii Bortsov! My key skills are Python, SQL, git. My journey in learning python began when Yandex launched "CodeRun" - a 3-month competition for 5 tracks (I chose the backend track) where the participants had to solve a lot of tasks related to the chosen track (backend track contained 54 tasks). During the "CodeRun", I was learning Python and SQL as many tasks were aimed to using some API, deploying local server or writing some tricky SQL queries. As a result, I ended on the 30th place with 51 solved problems.
I have quite good skills in creating Telegram bots and I had a lot of experience with the aiogram library. So far I have only implemented pet-projects, like Image Saver Bot, or bot for manaing tasks. Also as a school project, I created a simple website to memorize words, kanji, and kana for the Japanese language textbook "Читаем, пишем, говорим по-японски".

I have over 5+ years experience in competitive programming. My main achievments are: highest rating of International Master on Codeforces, 2nd degree diploma on the student Olympiad in programming (one of the toughest all-Russian competitive programming Olympiad).
In the university, my team managed to take 2nd place on ICPC quarterfinals and get 3rd degree diploma on ICPC semifinals. Unfortunately, we didn't get to the finals. But I am only first-year student, so I hope on continue polishing my competitive programming skills along with working on my projects.
